What is ChatGPT?

OpenAI, founded by Sam Altman, developed a model called ChatGPT, an open-source conversational AI system. It is powered by a neural network that has been trained on millions of conversations and is based on the GPT-3 model.

This AI model has been trained on hundreds of billions of words from the internet to generate text responses in a conversational way. It can be used for customer service, virtual assistants, and other applications.

Moreover, to ensure accuracy, ChatGPT underwent fine-tuning through supervised and reinforcement learning. To further improve the model, PPO (Proximal Policy Optimization) was used to build the ‘reward models.’

This training was done in partnership with Microsoft on their Azure supercomputing system, which helped in getting a cost-effective and speedy result.
