What is ChatGPT?

OpenAI, a research lab founded by Elon Musk and Sam Altman, has created ChatGPT, a conversational AI chatbot platform. This platform utilizes natural language processing (NLP) to comprehend and address inquiries from its users. It is built on the Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3 (GPT-3) model, which is the most extensive natural language model ever created. GPT-3 is trained on a colossal amount of data and can generate precise and human-like replies to open-ended inquiries.
